E-commerce

E-commerce is the activity of buying and selling goods and services through electronic medium. It basically uses internet for one or more part of its transactions.

Benefits of e-commerce

  1. International marketplace.
  2. Mass cutomization.
  3. Enables flexible working practices.
  4. 24/7 access to products to the consumers.
  5. Price comparision of the products.
  6. Facilitates delivery of public services.

Limitations of e-commerce

  1. Lack of sufficient system security, standards and communication protocol.
  2. Rapidly evolving and changing technology.
  3. Dependent on electronic process.
  4. A basic technical knowledge.
  5. Cost to access the internet.
